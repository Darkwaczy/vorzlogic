How It’s Work

Transplix ensures a seamless logistics process since 1948.
Here’s how we deliver your goods efficiently with real-time updates.

STEP 01
Received your product

At 1600h on Jan 01, 2020, we receive your product. Our team logs and verifies it for accuracy, initiating the process.

STEP 03
Package has been handed over

By 1800h, your package is handed over to our transport network. It’s securely transferred for optimized routing and tracking.

STEP 02
Warehousing

Jan 02, 2020, your package is stored in our secure warehouses. We manage inventory efficiently, ensuring readiness for dispatch.

STEP 04
